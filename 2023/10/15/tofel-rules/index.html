Tofel_rules

for administrations beginning July 26, 2023

  1. reading 30 grades

    20 tasks, 36 mins

    2 passages, 10 tasks for each

    from college textbooks about specific subjects

    exposition, argumentation, historical

    mins 1 task about passages structure: classification, compare, cause/effect, problem/solution

    single selection

    multi-selection

    insert passage

    further understanding

  2. listening, 30 grades

    28 tasks, 36 mins

    2 conversations, 5 tasks for each.

    3 lectures, 6 tasks for each

    lectures from real-world classes.

    conversations from students and professor or workers.

    single selection

    multi-selection

    sort problems

    classifications

  3. speaking, 30 grades

    4 tasks, 16 mins

    single speaking, speak

    complicated speaking, read, listen, speak

  4. writing, 30 grades

    2 tasks, 29 mins

    complicated writing

    from articles or listening materials writing

    Writing for an Academic Discussion

    respond to or build on ideas expressed in other text

    clearly state or elaborate on a position or argument

    show accurate and meaningful use of a range of grammatical structures and vocabulary in the academic context

within 2 hours
